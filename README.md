# Melange Strategies

## Strategies? What are you, some kind of hedge fund?

No, hedge funds have customers. We're a prop shop.

Well, prop shops have partners. We're more of a family office...

...with $0 billion AUM and counting.

## Why Melange?

It's kind of a mixed bag: we're based near the dunes of South Holland, we like spicy food, and we like to imagine that eating it will give us visions of the future and make us rich.

## What is this actually?

### Like, what are you even doing?

I'm an IT freelancer who's spent some time in the trading industry. My current clients aren't in that field, so keep my hand in, I play around with the sort of technology you use to connect to exchanges and make trades at ridiculously high speeds.

### If you can do all that, why don't you just actually get rich trading?

Well, the sophisticated tax law reason is that, as an American, if I own a trading firm here in the Netherlands, I get absolutely annihilated by US tax law thanks to the concept of a "Passive Foreign Investment Company" (PFIC); and my family's right to stay here is tied to the fact that I operate my own profitable Dutch corporation.

The slightly less sophisticated reason is I don't have enough money. You need a *lot* of money, not regular-person money, for the risks and overhead of a trading firm to make sense. So, instead, I offer these peculiar skills for hire to those who *do* have a lot of money.

### Why should I hire you?

I can help you build extremely reliable systems that move extremely large amounts of data, make extremely fast decisions, document things perfectly, and fix things when they break. If you want me to do this because you have a big pile of money and you want to make it bigger, I will help you in exchange for my own smaller (but still large by regular-person standards) pile of money. If you want me to do this for an interesting-enough art, science, or social project, or a cool startup, I will be much more flexible about the money.
